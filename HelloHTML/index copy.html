About

Welcome freshmans to NYU Abu Dhabi! As you begin your journey here, you’ll find that this campus is more than just a place to learn—it's a vibrant, diverse community where students from all over the world come together to share ideas, cultures, and experiences. Whether you’re navigating your first class, finding new friends, or exploring the UAE’s rich culture, you’re never alone. Remember, your time here is what you make of it, so embrace every opportunity, stay curious, and don’t hesitate to ask for help. This guide is your first step toward an unforgettable adventure!
